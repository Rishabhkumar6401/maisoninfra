Welcome to Sobha City - the premium residential project developed by Sobha Limited in Sector 108 of Gurgaon. With over 1,548 apartments ranging from 2, 3Â  & 4 bedrooms, Sobha City offers a spacious and luxurious living space equipped with modern amenities that cater to your every need.
